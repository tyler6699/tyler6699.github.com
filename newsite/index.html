The Careless Coder

I spend my days working as development manager, where I cannot be careless! In my spare time when I am not with family or running I love to play with pixels and game dev code.

Ryan Tyler

Release and Development Manager

Played Atari, Gameboy, Mega Drive and Amiga as a kid and have since loved gaming. Dabbled in code and pixels from 12 years old with Easy AMOS and DPaint II, since moved onto Java, Javascript and Unity.
I love 2D retro / pixel art games; try to enter game jams when I can and post tutorials on Wordpress. Moslty active on Twitter if you are interested in following.
